The essay made me rethink what a website can be beyond just something functional, like it doesn’t have to exist only to do one thing or guide people in a specific way. I like the idea of it being more of a space or experience where things feel a bit open and not totally defined. I imagine my own site as something that kind of grows and changes over time instead of staying the same, like I can keep adding to it and letting it shift as my ideas change. That’s why making it myself feels important, because I’m not locked into someone else’s structure, I get to decide how it works, what stays, what changes, and how it all comes together in a way that feels natural to me.
