I feel like a human-made object that brings out the best in me is my noise-canceling headphones. I sometimes have an issue with being in a space where there is a lot of noise and commotion as I get overstimulated really easily. My AirPods allow me to still be a part of the conversation while also canceling out most of the background noise. I also really like listening to music in them, and it allows me to think and process things more clearly. You can sometimes catch me walking around campus in them or even talking to friends with them in. I sometimes keep them in (playing no music) during direct conversations, and that allows me to focus more on the person speaking than what is going on around us. I feel like these devices allow me to be my unstressed, more relaxed best self both in class and outside of class. They act as a filter between me and the world. Its nice that with them instead of everything hitting me at once, I can control what I let in and what I tune out. The ability to control the sound around me makes a huge difference in how I experience everyday situations, especially in busy or chaotic environments. Without them I feel like I’m constantly trying to manage too many inputs at once. Which can be exhausting. With them, things feel quieter, more intentional, and easier to navigate. They don’t disconnect me from people, they actually help me show up better. I can listen better, and respond more thoughtfully. It’s almost like they create a small, personal space wherever I am. Like for instance if im in the middle of a crowded room, I can put them in and create a personal bubble. That space gives me the ability to slow down, think clearly, and be more like the version of myself I want to be.
